The Mountain Gorillas of Virunga National Park

The world’s entire population of endangered mountain gorillas live only in the Virunga, regarded as the home to around a third of those gorillas, which reside in the dense forests on the lush slopes of the Virunga volcanoes.

The Canopy Walkway

Suspended above a ravine in the lush montane rainforest of Nyungwe National Park, One of the oldest rainforests in Africa, Nyungwe is rich in biodiversity and spectacularly beautiful. The mountainous region with wildlife of a population of chimpanzees as well as species the L’Hoest’s monkeys and a variety of bird species

Akagera National Park

With the big 5. Lions, elephants, buffaloes, and leopards. There are hyenas, zebras, hippos, crocodiles, antelopes, cape elands, impalas, wide eyed bush-bays, warthogs, oribi, bush buck, Tsessebe ,sittatungas. There are over 100 elephants in the park today.

Visit Wonderful Rwanda

Discover the Land of a Thousand Hills

With so many natural wonders in Rwanda, it’s impossible not to enjoy an adventurous and exciting vacation you’ve always wanted.

Rwanda, renowned as ‘land of thousand hills’ is an impeccably beautiful country; characterized by fascinating savannah, undulating hills and deep valleys, abundant wildlife and natural aesthetics. Its capital city, Kigali is picturesque with many sights and delights.

Top Tour Destinations

  • Akagera National Park

    The Central Africa’s largest protected wetland and the last remaining refuge for savannah-adapted species in Rwanda.

  • The Canopy Walkway

    Suspended above a ravine in the lush montane rainforest of Nyungwe National Park, One of the oldest rainforests in Africa.

  • Virunga National Park

    The home to more mammal, bird and reptile species and the three extraordinary taxa of Great Apes.

  • The King's Palace

    A reconstruction of the traditional royal residence, the King’s Palace is a beautifully-crafted thatched dwelling shaped like a beehive.
