The Land of a Thousand Hills

Rwanda is lying south of the Equator in east-central Africa. Known for its breathtaking scenery, Rwanda is often referred to as “The land of a thousand hills”. The capital is Kigali, located in the centre of the country.

Rwanda is bounded to the north by Uganda, to the east by Tanzania, to the south by Burundi, and to the west by the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) and Lake Kivu, at the co-ordinates 2°00′S 30°0′E. At 26,338 square kilometres (10,169 sq mi).
